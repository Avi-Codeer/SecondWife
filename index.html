A Beloved Dining Destination in Saharsa

Second Wife Restaurant & Cafe is one of Saharsa's most popular dining destinations — a place where great food meets warm hospitality.

Whether you're planning a casual outing with friends, a family celebration, or a business meeting, our restaurant offers the perfect setting. We take pride in our carefully crafted menu featuring authentic Indian flavors, succulent kebabs, aromatic biryanis, and a wide range of fast-food favorites.
